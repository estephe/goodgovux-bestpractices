Our website serves many different audiences, how do we serve them all?

Plan

a) Figure out your audience

Do you have a handle on who your users are?

i. The first step to improving the user experience of your site is to figure out who the users are (or who you want them to be). There are several ways to get information about the users of your site. You can conduct  online surveys, focus groups, contextual interviews , individual interviews.
